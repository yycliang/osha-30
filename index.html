OSHA Instructor: John Baiardi

Email: IHORSE84@rcn.com

John attended the Hudson Valley Community College and graduated with an Associates Degree in Construction Technology. After graduation, he continued his work for a mason contracting company. In 1987, John started working as an assistant carpentry teacher for the NYC Department of Education, and has been working there for the last 31 years. In 2007, he became an OSHA Instructor and has been training construction workers in construction safety ever since.
